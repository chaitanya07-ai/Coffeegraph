Our Story

Indonesian Heritage,
Denver Soul

Coffeegraph is a Denver-based specialty coffee destination inspired by Indonesia's rich coffee heritage. We source premium beans from Indonesia and around the world, crafting beverages that blend tradition, innovation, and hospitality.

Our mission is to bring people together through exceptional coffee experiences, handcrafted drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere on Julian St.
